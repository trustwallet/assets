## Risk Assessment

This token uses USDT-related branding and naming that may mislead users into believing they are interacting with legitimate Tether USD (USDT).

Observed concerns:

- Uses USDT-related naming.
- May be confused with official Tether USD.
- Potential impersonation risk.
- Potential financial loss for users.

## Recommendation

Review this token and consider applying appropriate risk classification, warnings, or scam indicators.
